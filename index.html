What is "Whos a dog?"

Whos a good is dog is a data anytics platform that captures information on dogs that are available for adoption from volunteers that walk the dogs. After each walk volunteers leave a review for how the dog did oduring their time together. Reviews are in devleopment, however some sections include leash manners, interactions with others and energy levels.

This application is still in development by Stephen Hook as a ongoing project for a development of coding, but also develop this platform for acutal use to adopt dogs.
